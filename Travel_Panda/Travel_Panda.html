What it offers:

The St. Augustine Lighthouse is the most haunted place in St. Augustine. The lighthouse offers ghost tours in the dark. On this tour you can learn about the history and hauntings of the lighthouse. You can also investigate the lighthouse and search for ghost with equipment. People say that at the lighthouse keepers would hear footsteps, vioces, and lights would turn on and off. Also, workers have saw a man walking in the basment of the house where the lighthouse keepers lived.

  • explore the St. Augustine Pirate and Treasure Museum
